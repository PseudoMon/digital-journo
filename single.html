Digital Journo

A Digital Journal Theme

Wed, 10 February 2027

Digital Journo is a simple theme made for blogs or microblogs, based on old Japanese blogs. It's most suitable for blogs that have short, perhaps daily, posts, but can also be suited for anything.

Every post can have some extra info attached to it, which will be included under the post (in the main/archive page) and in the sidebar (in single post view page); useful if you want to include some status, like the book you're reading, or what you're eating at the day you're writing the post.

These pages are made by PseudoMon. For details of use, see the repo.
